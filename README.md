# taylor-swift-lyrics-llm
Topic modeling and sentiment analysis on Taylor Swift's lyrics across her 11 albums

## Introduction
This project aims to extract themes and analyze sentiments of all Taylor Swift’s song lyrics from
her 11 albums throughout the years. Each album has a distinct theme and was written in different
stages of her romantic life and music career reflecting her evolution as a person and as an artist.
It will be interesting to explore the hidden emotional patterns and themes in her songs and
understand how her life experience informs and influences her song writing.

Understanding the sentiments from texts can be challenging for non-native English speakers,
which is even harder for song lyrics. This project uses techniques like TF-IDF, Doc2V ec, Latent
Semantic Analysis (LSA), and zero-shot classification model for theme extraction and sentiment
analysis to deepen the appreciation of Taylor Swift’s songs and to provide a framework for
analyzing lyrics for other artists.

## Methods
The dataset is acquired from kaggle and includes all 274 songs across 64 albums from her 11
albums. The original kaggle dataset has duplicated albums and songs from different
re-recordings and versions and only original recording of each song is selected as the corpus of
this project. This leaves 169 songs from 11 albums.
